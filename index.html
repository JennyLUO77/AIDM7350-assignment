About me

Hi! I'm LUO Jinjin. A graduate student in AIDM from HKBU. Undergraduate is Digital Media Technology major in Interactive Design.

UI designer (maybe), fan of coffee and film photography.

For me, film is a special medium for carrying photographs. Unlike photographs taken directly from a digital camera, the special colours and graininess of film cannot be matched by digital, although film now also needs to be digitised for viewing.

My cameras

OLYMPUS XA2

It was introduced in 1980 as a stripped-down version of the XA. The Olympus XA2 features a four-element f/3.5 lens.XA2's exposure system favors wide depth-of-field, leaving the focus slider in the middle position generally results in in-focus shots. And since the slider slides into the middle position when the dust cover is closed.

OLYMPUS Trip 35

Trip's fantastic 40mm F/2.8 Zuiko lens delivers in spades. The lens is a front-focusing Tessar type lens, which means that it's very simple and very sharp, and it retains this sharpness edge to edge without chromatic aberration, spherical aberration, or any kind of distortion due to its simple optical formula and Olympus's masterful execution.
